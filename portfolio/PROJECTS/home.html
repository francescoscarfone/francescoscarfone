A website based on the community that populates the Milan Polytechnic. The project is presented as an archive that contains various unusual and peculiar, found by interviewing people, and digging through their bags. Each of these objects has a unique story worth showing and preserving. The goal is to tell the story of a community not through the visible but by using what cannot be seen, what lies beneath, reachable only by digging, in this case inside bags and backpacks. Stories that expose the funny, human and non-obvious side of people. The concept contrasts the meaning of archaeology and digging, with a modern plastic method of preservation, under vacuum, to keep each object and its history unchanged over time.

Soil is the basis and support of everything on earth. Factors such as urbanization,deforestation and intensive agriculture are suffocating it,preventing it from absorbing carbon from the atmosphere.In 2050,the death of soil is officially declared;completely barren,it is no longer capable of generating life,at least on its own. The Exhoil oil company,which has always exploited the soil's resources,decides to take action by stopping taking and starting to give.At the new Exhoil stations,gasoline pumps are replaced by respiration columns,which convert the carbon dioxide from human breath into carbon that nourishes the soil,thus generating for itan artificial respiration where human beings are the resource.

CERTOSA DISTRICT
TEAM

Federico Gajo,
Emiliano Garibaldi,
Nicola Maria Patitucci
The Certosa District is an urban redevelopment project of the companyReal Step. It needed a rebranding process that would best represent the required values. The proposed identity is based on the concept of assembling and giving new form. What is Certosa in fact, if not a space where ideas are born? Where precisely do ideas take shape? The starting point for the development of the identity was to synthesize the forms that characterize the district. Buildings are born from ideas,they are structures that enclose concepts:ideas give shape to Certosa,and at the same time,the forms of Certosa assemble with each other to create the district of tomorrow.Typography,as a tool forrepresenting concepts and ideas,dictates spaces,designs them. It gives form to Certosa.
